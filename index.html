Types of Stars:

Stars are huge balls of hot gas that produce light and heat. They are found throughout the universe and come in many different sizes and temperatures. Scientists study stars to learn more about how the universe works.

Star Type Colour Temperature (°C)
Blue Stars Blue 30,000°C or more
White Stars White 7,500–30,000°C
Yellow Stars Yellow 5,500–7,500°C
Orange Stars Orange 3,500–5,500°C
Red Stars Red 2,000–3,500°C
